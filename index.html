Our Brands

Interstuhl is an independent, owner-run family company.We are a German company, developing high quality chairs for offices and buildings, which we sell around the world.Our products are characterised by exceptional design, excellent ergonomics, state-of-the-art technology and a high degree of environmental awareness.“Seating knowledge: our advantage” is our motto.

Ditre Italia’s mission is to create and produce sofas and armchairs, turning high-quality manufacturing and design into values within everyone’s reach.DitreItalia has longstanding experience in the upholstered furniture sector. From when it was founded in 1976 by the De Marchi brothers to the sub-supplier period of the factory in San Martino di Colle Umberto, ongoing improvement in business performance has enabled the company to grow quickly. It has changed from an artisan business to the present-day company.

Newform Ufficio’s mission to build office furniture up to date, both with technology and human activities.Talent creativity and Italian design for a worldwide product with a metropolitan taste and environmentally friendly.Newform Ufficio’s aim is to equip spaces with ergonomic solutions enhancing the job experience.

Orrefors Kosta Boda AB is a Swedish design company that designs and makes high-quality utility glassware and art glass. They are the largest glassworks group in the Nordic countries with a history dating back to 1742.
